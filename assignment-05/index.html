Classic Buffalo Wings

Ingredients

  • Deep Fryer
  • 3 Quarts of vegetable Oil
  • 4lbs Party Style Chicken Wings
  • 1 Large Bottle of Franks Hot Sauce
  • 1 Stick of Butter

Directions

  1. Pour vegetable oil into deep fryer, till basket is fully submerged
  2. Heat deep fryer to 375 degrees
  3. Warm franks hot sauce on stove and mix with butter on lowest temperature
  4. Fill deep fryer basket with wings and let cook for minimum of 12 minutes
  5. When wings are cooked to satisfaction, mix wings with franks hot sauce in pan and eat em'
  6. They taste amazing don't they, you're welcome
