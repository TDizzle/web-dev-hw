About you instructor: Tristan Lilly
A well versed motorcycle rider and DIY mechanic.
This instructable is an easy way to help new motorcyclists take care of their beloved motorcycles.
An oil change is part of the basic maintenance needed to keep any gas engine running, and doing it yourself can save a lot of money in the long run.
You can learn about motorcycle maintenance and more from forums such as 600rr.net. This website happens to be brand and model specific, to the motorcycle listed in this instructable, but there are forums for every type of motorcycle and every brand.
